Terms - Delivery Address Extractor

The app only provides structured field extraction from submitted delivery text.

Users are responsible for providing lawful and appropriate input.

  • The app does not provide delivery guarantees.
  • The app does not judge whether an address can be delivered today.
  • The app does not track shipments.
  • The app does not contact couriers.
  • The app does not modify orders.
  • The app does not update external systems.
  • The app does not write customer service replies.

Extracted fields should be reviewed by a human or downstream system before operational use.
