Known Limitations

  • It only extracts explicitly stated delivery fields.
  • It may return null when information is missing.
  • It does not infer missing addresses, names, or phone numbers.
  • It does not call delivery systems.
  • It does not contact couriers.
  • It does not modify orders.
  • It does not judge delivery availability.
