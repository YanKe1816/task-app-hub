Privacy - Support Message Classifier

This privacy page applies only to Support Message Classifier.

  • No login required.
  • No user account required.
  • The app processes only text submitted by the user for the current request.
  • The app does not store submitted support messages long term.
  • The app does not sell user data.
  • The app does not use submitted content for advertising.
  • The app does not call external APIs.
  • The app does not contact customers.
  • The app does not update ticket systems or external records.
  • The app is read-only and has no side effects.

Privacy or data requests can be sent to sidcraigau@gmail.com.
