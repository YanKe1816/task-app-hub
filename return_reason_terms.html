Terms - Return Reason Normalizer

The app only provides structured return reason normalization.

  • The app does not approve returns.
  • The app does not reject returns.
  • The app does not assign responsibility or blame.
  • The app does not provide legal, financial, or customer service advice.
  • The app does not replace human review.
  • The app does not contact customers.
  • The app does not modify order records.
  • The app does not execute external actions.

Users are responsible for reviewing outputs before using them in business workflows.

The app is provided as-is.
